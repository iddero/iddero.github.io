VERSO INDOOR doubles as a modern indoor monitor for your door phone system. Receive incoming calls from your door entry panel and enjoy full-color digital video and high quality audio with acoustic echo cancellation.

The choice is yours

The application program includes preset configurations for MURA IP, 2N, DoorBird, and FERMAX MEET door stations, and allows manual configuration of SIP-based door stations from other manufacturers.
