4.3” KNX capacitive touch panel with IP connectivity

now, twice as good

Anytime, anywhere

VERSO+IP 2 has everything you love about the PLUS X Award Winner VERSO 2, and adds an IP connection for seamless remote control from smartphones and tablets, in a practical and straightforward way.

  • Mobile apps available for iOS and Android
  • Both direct (LAN) connection and cloud-based operation are supported; no additional configuration required
  • Communications are fully encrypted and 100% secure
  • No licenses required

Multiply the possibilities

VERSO+IP 2 has everything you need for your next KNX project: time schedules, alarm monitoring, an internal scene controller, two independent thermostats, built-in temperature sensor, four multifunction inputs, integrated KNX bus coupler...

...and much more! We’ve redesigned the product's “guts” to multiply its capabilities, and we have many surprises coming up for you.

.

Let's face it: it's a joy to see

The most attractive visualisation on the market is now twice as powerful: up to 96 components spread across 12 fully configurable pages. Don't leave anything out.

And of course, with a user interface that’s polished down to the last detail: a wide variety of advanced widgets, customizable background images on all pages, favorites section, touch gestures, dark mode, and much more.
