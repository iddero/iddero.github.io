4.3” KNX capacitive touch panel

now, twice as good

You already know Iddero VERSO, right?

And if not, you should.

The best 4.3” KNX touch panel is now twice as powerful. Create the perfect visualisation, with up to 96 components spread across 12 fully configurable pages.

And of course, with a user interface that’s polished down to the last detail: a wide variety of advanced widgets, customizable background images on all pages, favorites section, touch gestures, dark mode, and much more.

Beauty is (also) on the inside

You not only get the most attractive visualisation on the market. You also get a host of functionality: time schedules, alarm monitoring, an internal scene controller, two independent thermostats, built-in temperature sensor, four multifunction inputs, integrated KNX bus coupler...

...and much more! We’ve redesigned the product's “guts” to multiply its capabilities, and we have many surprises coming up for you.

.

Total Flexibility

Iddero VERSO 2 is available in white or black GLASS finishes, and can be installed in portrait or landscape mode. Installation is possible on solid walls or drywall, using standard wall mounting boxes.

Or you can install it totally flush with the wall, using flush mounting adapter E-F401.
