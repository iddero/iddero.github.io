Terraços de São Pedro do Estoril

Location and project type

Residential complex of 7 exclusive homes and private grounds in São Pedro do Estoril, just 25 km away from Lisbon, Portugal, with fantastic sea views.

Description

"Terraços de São Pedro do Estoril" is a private complex comprising 7 luxury single family houses, located in one of the best areas of the Portuguese coast.

These houses have been carefully designed to offer quality living spaces to the owners. Arranged over three floors, each house includes a private elevator, and enjoys excellent sea views. An antiseismic structure made of reinforced concrete, excellent thermal and acoustic insulation, and premium finishes and equipment are just some of their features.

An HVAC system based on an air­/water heat pump is used for hot water and central heating, and for air cooling and distribution, thus allowing for significant savings of gas and electricity.

Indoor common areas include an auditorium, gymnasium, locker rooms, and sauna. Outdoor facilities include spacious green areas and a large swimming pool.

Each of these exclusive homes is equipped with an Iddero HC2L-KNX touch panel, which allows full control over the home automation system with a user-friendly, intuitive interface, either through the integrated 10.4" touch display or from a smartphone or tablet.
