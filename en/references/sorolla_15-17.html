Sorolla 15-17

Can you imagine living in Málaga, by the sea?

In a city that was recently chosen by InterNations as the best city to live in for expats, and by Forbes as the best alternative capital city in the world.

In the exclusive district of El Limonar, the best residential area in the city, just 100 meters from the beach, but also a few minutes' walk from the historic center of Málaga and natural parks like El Morlaco and Gibralfaro.

In an exclusive development of 10 luxury homes, designed by Klic Arquitectos and inspired by the Curutchet House by the prestigious Swiss architect Le Corbusier, and which recently received a Special Mention of the Jury at the XVII Málaga Architecture Awards.

With the best finishes and qualities, and exceptional facilities including a swimming pool, landscaped common areas, a social club, and private terraces.

Some may wonder... “what else could you wish for?”

Well, we have the answer: best-in-class home automation, with control of lighting, blinds, and air conditioning, technical alarms (flood and smoke detection), and full door phone integration. Everything is managed and controlled from an Iddero HC3-KNX 7" touch panel with GLASS finish; remote access is possible from smartphones and tablets through the Iddero Mobile app.

A technological solution that meets—and exceeds—all expectations. It was the only sensible choice.
