Dunique Marbella

Picture this.

You wake up to the soothing sound of the sea in the background. It’s 9:00 a.m. You get up and make yourself a coffee.

There’s a light breeze, so you decide to enjoy your coffee in your solarium, in the jacuzzi, taking in the stunning sea views. The beach is less than 100 meters away.

Later, you’ll head over to the private club for a workout at the gym, and maybe swing by the spa afterwards—or swim a few laps in the suspended pool. But you don’t want to linger too long, because at noon you’ve got a wine tasting with friends in your private garden.

As you walk into the gym, it hits you—you should probably make sure the house is at the perfect temperature by the time your guests arrive. No problem. You grab your phone, open the app, and schedule the air conditioning to turn on soon, just the way you like it. By the time you’re back, everything will be just right.


Sounds good? Well, that could be just an ordinary day for the owners of one of the 32 luxury villas or 64 high-end apartments and penthouses at Dunique Marbella. The villas, with 3 or 4 bedrooms, offer up to 350 m² of interior space and private gardens of up to 380 m². The apartments and penthouses stretch up to 450 m² built, with outdoor areas of up to 250 m².

They call it “sustainable luxury.” Because not only do these homes feature the highest-quality finishes and every comfort you could ask for—they’re also energy-efficient, with BREEAM certification.


So.

What do all the owners of these homes have in common?

They know quality when they see it.

That’s why every home is equipped with Iddero HC3L-KNX 10.1” touch panels, with remote access and full integration with the video intercom system. And of course, additional VERSO 2 touch panels in every room.

What else did you expect?
